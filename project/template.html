Playlab Starter Kit

A set of apps to get your school started.

Over the course of the 2025-26 school year, 45 fellows from across 16 CIOB schools built a collection of AI tools to deepen thinking, follow student curiosity, and strengthen relationships.

From over 100 apps, this is a small starter set you can use today and remix tomorrow. Each app works out of the box. Open it, try it out, then make a copy and shape it to your school.
